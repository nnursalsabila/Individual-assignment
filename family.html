I want to share a glimpse of the most important part of my life- my family. There are six people in my family - me, my mum and dad, my two brothers and our little sister. They have always instilled in me and my siblings the importance of education, hardwork and perseverance. I am beyond grateful for their love and support, and I know that I can always count on them. My relationship with my parents are great.
